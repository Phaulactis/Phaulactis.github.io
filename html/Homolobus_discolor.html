Homolobus discolor

Information Gallery Key

Description

A distinctive species, easily recognized by the dark coloration of the head, thorax, mesonotum and apical part of hind tibia. Most of the legs and part of mesosternum and propodeum orange to yellowish brown. See “key characters”.

Ecology

A thelytokous species with two generations each year. All known hosts are Geometrid moths.

Distribution

Known from much of the palaearctic. Distribution in Sweden is porly understood but it is known from southern and central parts of the country.
