Homolobus truncator

Information Gallery Key

Description

Distinguishable most reliably from similar species by the lack of any teeth or lamella on the tarsal claws, but this can be dificult without high magnification. See “key characters”.

Ecology

Known to have multiple generations each year. All known hosts are Geometrid or Noctuid moths.

Distribution

A holarctic species, but rather rare in the palaearctic. In Sweden has finds been made in Scania and on Gotland.
