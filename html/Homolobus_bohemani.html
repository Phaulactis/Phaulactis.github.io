Homolobus bohemani

Information Gallery Key

Description

A rare species recognized by the unusally elongated face (malar space equal to or greater than the width of the mandible). Coloration often similar to Homolobus annulicornis but females have a much shorter ovipositor. See “key characters”.

Ecology

No hosts are known.

Distribution

Infrequently found throughout the palaearctic. In Sweden has specimens been found in Småland and Norbotten, suggesting a nationwide distribution.
